# **SOFTWARE (PRD)**

## **Offline Visitor Data Capture Platform**

---

## **1\. Introduction**

The goal of this platform is to help physical businesses automatically collect customer details when people enter their shops, without **stress**, **delays**, or **complicated steps**.

The system must work well in low network areas, use very little data, and be easy for everyone to use.

---

## **2\. Product Goals**

The software must:

* Help businesses collect customer **names**, **phone numbers**, and **emails**  
* Work with tap devices at shop entrances and tables  
* Load very fast on any phone  
* Use very little internet data  
* Respect customer privacy  
* Help businesses send messages and promotions  
* Show clear reports to business owners  
* Help the company manage devices, payments, and users

---

##  **Use Dynamic NFC Links**

**What it is:**  
 Instead of encoding the actual final URL into the NFC chip, you encode a **short, fixed URL that points to a server** you control. The server then **redirects to the real page**.

**How it works in practice:**

1. The NFC chip stores:  
    `https://tap.yourplatform.com/abc123`

2. When a customer taps, the phone opens this URL.

3. Your server checks which business this NFC belongs to and redirects to:

   * Business A: `https://platform.com/business-a/form`

   * Business B (later): `https://platform.com/business-b/form`

**Result:**

* You can **change the target link anytime** without touching the NFC chip.

* One NFC plate can serve **multiple businesses over its lifetime**.
